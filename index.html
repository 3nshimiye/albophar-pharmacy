Welcome to Socius Pharmacy

A partner you can trust for your healthcare and wellness.

About Us

At Socius Pharmacy, we are committed to providing you with trusted healthcare products and personalized services. Our mission is to deliver high-quality medications and tailored care to support your well-being and help you lead a healthier life.

With a team of experienced and dedicated healthcare professionals, we are here to assist you with all your pharmaceutical needs — offering expert advice, reliable service, and a customer-first approach you can depend on.

Featured Products & Services

OTC Medications

Browse our selection of over-the-counter medications for your everyday health needs — from pain relief and cold remedies to digestive aids and allergy solutions. These products do not require a medical prescription. However, our team is always available to offer guidance if needed, ensuring you choose the right medication for your symptoms.

Prescription Medications

Access the medications your healthcare provider has prescribed for you, safely and efficiently. Backed by extensive expertise, we dispense a wide range of prescription drugs strictly in accordance with your prescriber’s orders. Our pharmacists are here to guide you on proper usage, potential side effects, and refill options — ensuring your treatment is safe, smooth, and effective.

Wellness Products

Find vitamins, supplements, and wellness products designed to support your long-term health. Whether you're looking to boost immunity, improve energy, or support joint health, we’ve got trusted brands you can rely on.

Personal Care Essentials

Explore a wide variety of personal care items — from skincare and hygiene products to baby care and grooming essentials. All the everyday basics your household needs, in one convenient place.

Medical Devices & Home Health Supplies

Monitor your health from the comfort of your home with our range of medical devices. We offer trusted equipment such as blood pressure monitors, glucose meters, digital thermometers, oximeters, and first aid kits among others, our team is trained to empower you with the basic knowledge for you to use and handle the device for a long-term effective use — empowering you to manage your health independently and effectively.

Refill & Medication Management

Easily manage your prescriptions with our refill and medication support services. We assist with tracking refills, providing reminders, and offering professional advice to help you stay on top of your treatment plan.
